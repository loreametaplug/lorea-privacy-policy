Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Lorea Profile Manager ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your information.

Information Collection

Lorea Profile Manager collects and stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • Service names
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) secret keys

All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage system (chrome.storage.sync) and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Use of Information

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Generating time-based one-time passwords (TOTP) for two-factor authentication
  • Displaying your saved profiles within the extension
  • Syncing your data across your Chrome browsers when you're signed into Chrome

Data Storage and Security

We take your security seriously:

  • All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API
  • No data is ever transmitted to external servers
  • Data syncing only occurs through your Chrome account's secure sync feature
  • The extension operates entirely offline

Third-Party Access

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to any third parties. Your data remains strictly within your browser's secure storage.

Data Retention

Your data remains stored in your browser until you choose to remove it using the extension's interface or uninstall the extension.

User Rights

You have complete control over your data. You can:

  • View all stored information within the extension
  • Delete individual profiles at any time
  • Remove all data by uninstalling the extension

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
